🐾 About Careful Paws

Careful Paws was founded to provide loving care for pets when families are away. As a pet lover myself, I wanted to create a space where animals feel safe, happy, and part of the family.

Every decision — from play areas to meal plans — is designed with the pet’s comfort and well-being in mind. Our mission is to make pet boarding stress-free for both animals and owners.

Running this kennel has taught me patience, attention to detail, and how small touches make a big difference — and I hope this site shows our commitment to care and love.
